  1. Why the site is in English only? I prefer to read Chinese. [Top]
    By analyzing the visitors of this site, I found the majority (87.9%) of them come from U.S. and Japan. They cannot read Chinese. Although I can create two versions of the same site, I cannot bear the the overhead to keep the two versions synchronized. Above all, most visitors of my site can read English nowadays.
  2. Why the site contains only text and photos? You know, pictures, icons and flashes make a web more beautiful. [Top]
    I am not a visual designer, so my graphics would look crummy anyway. Since this web site is totally created by myself, the text-only nature is suitable for the current situation. I believe content is the most important for a web site instead of format and appearance.
  3. Where is your web server? I mean the physical location of your web site? [Top]
    I rented 60M disk space from an ISP. According to their Ad, the server is physically located in the Data Center of China Telecom, 14 Floor, 333 Wu Sheng Rd, Shanghai, China.
  4. Why do you create this web site? [Top]
    This is a normal homepage. The different is, I registered the domain and paid for web hosting services. I created a web site focusing on technology 4 years ago when I was in university. Now, I don't want to show off technology any more. This is the site that solely introduce myself. Maybe this is the most decent information source on the Internet about Jian Shuo Wang. I have many friends and I told them to come to this site to check latest information about me, so I don't need to send them individual emails.

  7. Why don't you use ASP or CGI on your web site? Do you know how to use it?
    Certainly I am very skillful on ASP and database stuff. I have been supporting ASP for about two years. The reason I don't use any ASP is simple: I didn't pay for it and ISP only provide static web page storage for me. Currently, based on the fact that the page hits per day is still very low, there is not need to automate anything, I don't need a dynamic pages now.
