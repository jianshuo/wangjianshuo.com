The theater Scene in Tel Aviv

Before I went to Israel, I knew nothing about theatre in today Tel aviv, also I do think Tel Aviv will be a dangerous city, it is easy to see the news from TV station in China about Tel Aviv and Jerusalem, but my thought is wrong, Tel Aviv is such a beautiful and peaceful city, walking on the street, you can feel the wonderful views from everywhere, I like Jewish people here, they are very friendly, you can hear people say " Ni Hao" to you on the street.

About the theater scene here in Tel Aviv, it surprises me so much, I never think the theater here is so strong, all the theater are full, people enjoy the theater very much, Cameri and the Israel national theater are so great, they have lots of repertoire programe, I saw three plays from Cameri thaeter, the municipal theater company from Tel Aviv, their plays are really great, amazing and wonderful, I almost fell in love with this theater immediately when I just saw the first play " Requiem" which is adapted from three short stories of Chehov, I love this play very much, great actors, great writing, great set and wonderful direct, I like the way which the director who directs the play, like painting but with such a deep feeling, the sad feeling from the thought of Jewish history and their lives here. After I saw the HAMLET in Cameri, I almost want to stay in this theater for a long time, such a wonderful show with lots of amazing thoughts, the artists here are very lucky, they have really good audience, and they love each other, it is such a good situation for theater, a kind of traditional and old art style.

Compare to many theater companies from all over world, Tel Aviv has a really good situation for theater, in Cameri, each night, they will have around 6 or 7 shows, most the show are full, in the new cultural center, there are five different size stage for different shows, good qulity for these shows.

Just something about theater in Tel Aviv to creat my blog, I wish that I can insist to write my blog daily, will try that. Thanks for reading.
