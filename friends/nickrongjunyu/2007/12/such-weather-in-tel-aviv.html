Such an interesting weather in Tel Aviv

The weater in Tel Aviv is amazing, the day before yesterday was a wonderful day, the sky is blue without cloud, and the air wa fresh and warm, people can just wear T-shirt to walk on the street, even all that day I stayed in the theater to watch many show from fringe but I still could feel the wonderful weather outside of the theater.

But yesterday the weather was changed quickly, Tel Aviv welcame its sand storm, you can not see the city clear, everywhere it looked like in a foggy view, and also you can breath the really bad air with sand, walking along the beach, the wave was heavy and big, you can not see the sky, and also your mouth will fill with some sands, yesterday I had a great walk in old Yafa city, just several people in the street, the wind was very strong, I even could not keep steady when I stood in the front the old Yafa, all the old city seemed in empty, just some cats sat in the center gerden to have a wonderful meeting and also they didnot feel scare about tourists, anyway yesterday was very similar with Beijing in such a bad weather to have sand storm, even much worse than Beijing, for Tel-Aviv is really close to desert, the driver of taxi who told me, in Dec., sometimes Tel Aviv will have such kind of bad weather.

But today the weather changed again, in the morning I found the air become to fresh again, and it was rain, such a heavy rain, and the temperature became lower, somewhat cold outside, it looks like the spring time in shanghai with rainy day, but the beach is so charm today, and the Med sea became to blue again, still windy and rainy, but much better than yesterday. And the shower came again and again, you never know when it will be shower, just came suddenly.

Since the day before yesterday, just in three days, I had experienced three different topical days in Tel Aviv, a city nearby sea and desert, which can bring any topical weather to you, maybe that is the somewhat interesting part of Tel Aviv.
