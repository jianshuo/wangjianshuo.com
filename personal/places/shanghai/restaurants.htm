Bo Wang Po Dining-Room (Jin Shuo's Choice in Xu Jia Hui)

It is a Henan restaurant hosted by people coming from the city of Nan Yang, Henan Province. It is ideal for dinner after shopping in Xu Jia Hui. You can try Hui Noodle and Liang Pi there.
