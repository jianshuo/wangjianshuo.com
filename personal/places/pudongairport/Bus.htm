The tickets

You can buy tickets at the Bus Ticket Counter in the Arrival Hall, near gate 12

Or you can buy the ticket when you get on board. There is no difference where to buy the tickets.

The stop


The station of Airport Bus is right outside the International Arrival Hall (At Gate 16, Floor One). When you step out of the Arrival Hall (for International flights), you can see this large yellow/blue sign. Wait for bus there.


People waiting for Bus Line #3.

The bus


It is very comfortable to take airport buses. The picture is taken inside the bus. There are 39 seats per bus. Currently, in 2002, only 30-40% seats were occupied on buses to Pudong Airport and 100% seats were occupied on buses leaves Pudong airport.


Take a look at the seats.
