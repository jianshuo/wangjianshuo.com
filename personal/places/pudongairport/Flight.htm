There is no much information about domestic flights from Shanghai Airport to other cities in China (in English). I have worked with a ticket booking company to list the most updated airline information. It is cool that you can even book online - not really e-commerce, just send the information and they can arrange sending the tickets to you.

Please note: There are two airports in Shanghai

SHA - Shanghai Hong Qiao Airport. Most demotic flights will use this airport
PVG - Shanghai Pudong Airport. ALL international flights (and Hongkong, Macao, Taiwan flights) use this airport.

Airport bus #1 operates between the two airport.
