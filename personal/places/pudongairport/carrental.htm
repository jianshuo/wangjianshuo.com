Is there any car rental at Pudong airport

Hi
 
I am try to serach car rental at Pudong international airport. I come across your web page. It is helpful.  
 
Could you tell me is there any car rental for self driving? If there is, would you knoe what are the company name, phone number, and opening hours?

Juliet

A: Hi Juliet,
 
There is car rental services at Pudong Airport. Here is the information:
 
Name: Ji-an Car Rental Corp.
Tel: +86-21-68346668 (Pudong Airport Branch)
       +86-21-62291119 (Headquarter)
Location: Near Gate 7, Domestic Arrival Hall, Pudong Airport.
Business Hour: 8:30 AM - 5:00 PM, Monday - Friday
Website (Chinese) http://www.angel-rental.com/angel/neiye_shouxu.htm
 
You can contact them directly if you want to rent a car. As I know, they are the only car rental provider in Pudong airport.
 
I have talked with them. Please check if you have a valid driver's license in China. It is not allowed to drive without a China's driver's license.
 
If you are holding U.S. driver's license, you can get a temporary China driver's license at the Security Administration at Hong Qiao Airport (Tel: +86-21-6269508)
 
Note: Traveler's visa L is not permitted to drive in China.
 
You can get the car if you provide the China's driver license or temp driver's license and credit after in minutes. P.S.
