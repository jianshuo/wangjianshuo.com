Airport Construction Fee

Airport construction fee is charged for all passengers. It is 50 RMB for domestic flight and 90 RMB for international flight. You need to buy the tickets at the counter or using the automatic selling machines.


Airport Construction Fee Ticketing at the departure hall.


You can also find about 6 automatic selling machines. It accept 100 RMB and 50 RMB bill and output  the magnetic card.


You need to insert magnetic card to pass the channel. The machine will print receipt for you at the other end of it.
