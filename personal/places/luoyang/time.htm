Luoyang Time

 

Time zone: GMT +8 hours

There is only one time zone in China. No matter where you are, from the east most Shanghai to west most Xin Jiang. All of China observes Beijing Time, GMT+8.

Day light saving: No

China doesn't observe daylight saving time in the summer as some countries do. There is a short period of time when China apply day light saving from 1986-1991 though. This adjustment is abandoned in the year of 1992.

This year on Chinese Lunar Calendar: The year of horse

Starting 2/12/2002, Chinese people entered the year of horse. (Last year is the year of snake, staring January 24, 2001, the next year will be the year of sheep.) Everyone has a sign that is related to the Lunar year. Find your sign here.
