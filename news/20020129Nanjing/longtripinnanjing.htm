Long trip to Nanjing

January 29, 2002, Hilton Hotel, Nanjing

At the time you see this page, I should be in Nanjing and will stay in Nanjing for about 7 days.

I am going to Nanjing to deliver the Microsoft Development Management Advanced Training this whole week. Again, I will take train No. T712 to Nan Jing (SH14:10->NJ17:20) and return via T713(NJ13:01->SH16:12). To check the training schedule, visit Nan Jing Railway Station Web Site [Chinese web site].

February 4, 2002, Hilton Hotel, Nanjing

The training is completed successfully. It turned out to be a great success that the very satisfaction rate reaches as high as 50% and overall satisfaction is 100%. What a great number!! I am relaxed now and will return Shanghai happily today.

February 4, 2002, Shanghai

5 or 6 bags on shoulder, a big box full of books in hand, I returned to Shanghai office at last.... What a great journey!
